Top 5 Hotels in the World

1. Burj Al Arab, Dubai

Known as the world's only 7-star hotel, Burj Al Arab offers luxury beyond imagination with stunning views of the Arabian Gulf. The Burj Al Arab (Arabic: برج العرب, lit. 'Arab Tower') is a luxury hotel in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Developed and managed by Jumeirah, it is one of the tallest hotels in the world, although 39% of its total height is made up of non-occupiable space..

2. The Ritz Paris, France

A symbol of luxury in the heart of Paris, The Ritz offers opulent accommodations and a rich history of famous guests

3. The Four Seasons, Bora Bora

With its stunning overwater bungalows and breathtaking views, The Four Seasons in Bora Bora is a tropical paradise.

4. The St. Regis, New York

Located in Midtown Manhattan, The St. Regis offers luxurious accommodations with personalized butler service.

5. Aman Tokyo, Japan

A serene oasis in the bustling city, Aman Tokyo combines traditional Japanese design with modern luxury.
